1. pretrained_plome: the pretrained plome model
2. train.txt: train data.
The training data is composed of 10K manually annotated samples from SIGHAN 2013-2015, and 271K automatically generated samples from https://aclanthology.org/D18-1273/, it is available from https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aip_siFdXynxMz6-2iopWvJqr5jtUu3F/view?usp=sharing
3. test.txt: Sighan15 test data (only positive sentences). Due to the copyright issue, please visit the SIGHAN officail website to obtain the whole dataset.
Sighan15 official website: http://ir.itc.ntnu.edu.tw/lre/sighan8csc.html
Sighan14 official website: http://ir.itc.ntnu.edu.tw/lre/clp14csc.html
Sighan13 official website: http://ir.itc.ntnu.edu.tw/lre/sighan7csc.html
